Why does password strength matter?

Using the same password repeatedly across multiple sites might be convenient, but it also introduces a huge security risk. Your email account, social media, bank account, and other services could be compromised if someone discovered your password.

How do password strength testers work?

A strength tester measures how long it would take someone to brute force an attack using every possible letter, number, and special character combination until they crack it. This helps you choose the strongest possible password.

What makes a strong password?

Strong passwords are long, unique, and complex. They should include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid common words, personal information, or predictable patterns.
